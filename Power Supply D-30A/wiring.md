# Wiring & Safety — D-30A Power Supply

- This module is rated up to 30A — handle with care.
- Connections:
  - Ensure polarity and use adequately sized wires and secure terminals for high current.
  - Use proper fuses or breakers when testing high-current outputs.
- Safety:
  - Avoid short circuits; use current-limited bench supplies for initial testing.
  - Verify heatsinking and cooling for continuous high-current operation.

> Note: Treat this module as a potential high-energy device — follow best practices for wiring and testing.
