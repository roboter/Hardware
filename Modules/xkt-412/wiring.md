# Wiring — XKT‑412 Wireless Power Module

Typical connections:
- Transmitter:
  - VIN -> 5 V supply
  - GND -> ground
  - Coil -> transmitter coil (built-in on module)
- Receiver:
  - Coil -> receiver coil
  - OUT -> regulated output (5 V) to device
  - GND -> ground

Notes:
- Align transmitter and receiver coils for best transfer efficiency.
- Start tests at low current and monitor temperature; use a stable 5 V supply.
