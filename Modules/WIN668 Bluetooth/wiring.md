# Wiring — WIN668 Bluetooth Audio Module

Typical pins (7‑pin header):
- VCC: 3.7 V (battery) or 5 V (regulated)
- GND: ground
- L: Left audio output
- R: Right audio output
- MUTE: mute control
- LED: status LED (through resistor)
- KEY: external key / control input

Notes:
- Connect audio outputs to amplifier or headphone outputs through coupling capacitors as required.
- Confirm supply voltage and polarity before connecting to speakers.
